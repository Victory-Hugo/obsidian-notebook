摘要

Mitochondrial origin of the matrilocal Mosuo people in China

“matrilocal population in China” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 母系社会群体“The origins of the population and their matrilocal tradition remain unknown.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 人口的起源及其母系传统仍然未知。

“women remain with their families after marriage” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 妇女婚后仍与家人在一起“However, the haplotype diversity of this population is not lower than the neighboring patrilocal populations, indicating that the Mosuo started practicing matrilocality at least after the Paleolithic Age.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 但该人群的单倍型多样性并不低于邻近的父系社会人群，表明摩梭人（Mosuo）至少在旧石器时代之后就开始实行母系生活。

“A previous haplogroup frequency clustering study indicated that the Mosuo are closest to the Naxi people, but the network analysis of individual sequence haplotypes presented herein shows that most Mosuo lineages cluster with Pumi lineages.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 先前的单倍群频率聚类研究表明摩梭人与纳西人最接近，但本文提出的个体序列单倍型的网络分析表明大多数摩梭人谱系与普米人谱系聚类。“they started to practice matrilocality several thousand years ago.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 他们在几千年前就开始实行母居制。

介绍

“The Mosuo, the last living matrilocal population in Mainland China (Gottner-Abendroth 1999), have a population of around 53,000 (2010 census), mostly distributed around Lugu Lake, on the border of Yunnan and Sichuan provinces” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 摩梭人是中国大陆最后的母系人口（Gottner-Abendroth 1999），人口约 53,000 人（2010 年人口普查），主要分布在云南省和四川省交界的泸沽湖周边。

“most present-day populations have been practicing patrilocality (i.e. a practice in which females are exchanged frequently among populations) for many years, maternal lineages form very complex networks, making it difficult to trace the matrilineal history of these populations.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 当今大多数人口多年来一直实行父系制（即女性在人口之间频繁交换的做法），母系血统形成非常复杂的网络，使得追踪这些人口的母系历史变得困难。

“The present language of the Mosuo belongs to TangutQiang branch of the Sino-Tibetan language family. Ethnographic studies suggest that the Mosuo were believed to be descendants of the ancient Qiang tribes.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 13) 摩梭人现用的语言属汉藏语系西夏羌语族。民族志研究表明，摩梭人被认为是古代羌族部落的后裔。

“They originally lived in Northwest China, and some migrated to Southwest China about 2700 years ago, where they diversified into several branches. Among these branches, the Tangut-Qiang is most similar to the ancient Qiang” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 14) 他们最初生活在中国西北地区，大约2700年前，一些人迁徙到中国西南地区，并在那里分化成多个分支。在这些支系中，西夏羌语与古羌语最为相似。

“A study of Y-STR polymorphisms among five Chinese minorities shows that the Tibetan and Mosuo peoples are closest in paternal lineage (Yang et al. 2004).” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 14) 对中国五个少数民族的 Y-STR 多态性研究表明，藏族和摩梭人的父系血统最接近（Yang et al. 2004）。

“previous study of the Mosuo people suggests that they have various paternal origins, and therefore cannot identify a definitive source of genetic origin” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 14) 先前对摩梭人的研究表明，他们有不同的父系起源，因此无法确定遗传起源的明确来源。

“The maternal lineages of the Mosuo bear the strongest resemblance with those found in Naxi (Wen et al. 2004b).” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 14) 摩梭人的母系血统与纳西族的母系血统最为相似（Wen et al. 2004b）。

方法

“Peripheral blood samples from 47 Mosuo individuals, 14 Naxi individuals, and 18 Pumi individuals from various villages were collected for this study, with an approval from the Ethics Committee of the Fudan School of Life Sciences.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 14) 本研究经复旦大学生命科学学院伦理委员会批准，采集了来自不同村庄的47名摩梭人、14名纳西族和18名普米族人的外周血样本。

MtDNA HVS-1 区域测序

“All the HVS-1 sequences of the Mosuo people in this study are included in GenBank (HM215521-67).” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 15) 本研究中摩梭人的所有HVS-1序列均收录在GenBank中（HM215521-67）。

数据分析

“Haplotype diversity was calculated using Arlequin software (Version 3.11” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 15) 使用Arlequin软件（版本3.11）计算单倍型多样性。

“Networks of mtDNA HVS-1 motifs were constructed by the median-joining method (Bandelt et al. 1999) using Network version 4.5.1.0” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 15) mtDNA HVS-1 基序的网络是使用 Network version4.5.1.0 通过中值连接方法（Bandelt 等人，1999）构建的。

结果

“However, we found that the mtDNA HVS-1 haplotype diversity of Mosuo (0.9843) is not always lower than those of surrounding patriarchal populations, but even higher than those of the Naxi and Pumi” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 15) 然而，我们发现摩梭人的mtDNA HVS-1单倍型多样性（0.9843）并不总是低于周边父系人群，甚至高于纳西族和普米族。“Mosuo are considered intermediate among comparative population” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 15) 摩梭人被认为是相对人口中的中间人。

“There may be three mechanisms for a matrilocal population that would result in a population not exhibiting low mtDNA diversity: large population size, frequent admixture with other populations, and short matrilocality history” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 15) 母系种群可能存在三种机制导致种群不表现出低线粒体DNA多样性。

  1. 种群规模大
  2. 与其他种群频繁混合
  3. 母系历史短。

“The last possible explanation for this pattern is that the Mosuo might have switched from patrilocality to matrilocality very recently, and that their matrilocal society has not yet left visible effects on their genetic structure.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 16) 对于这种模式的最后一种可能的解释是，摩梭人可能最近才从父系转变为母系，而且他们的母系社会尚未对其遗传结构产生明显的影响。

“indicating that the Mosuo may not be a population that has existed since Paleolithic Age, as previously suggested.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 16) 表明摩梭人可能不像之前所说的那样是自旧石器时代以来就存在的人群。

“The six major mtDNA haplogroups (B4, B5a, C, D, D5a, and F1b) encompass over 80% of the Mosuo samples.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 17) ==六个主要 mtDNA 单倍群（B4、B5a、C、D、D5a 和 F1b）涵盖了超过 80% 的摩梭样本==。

“Most populations are scattered all over the network, indicating high diversity in maternal lineages as well as an ancient common ancestors for Sino-Tibetan populations.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 17) 大多数人群分散在整个网络中，这表明母系谱系具有高度多样性，并且汉藏人群拥有古老的共同祖先。

“If the Mosuo (M) was closest to the Naxi (X), as we previously showed, or shared or connected haplotypes are expected between the two populations in Figure 2, we would expect them to share a recent common ancestor. However, no such relationship was seen in the network, strongly arguing against the assumption that the Mosuo and Naxi share a recent common ancestor in their maternal lineages.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 17) 如果摩梭人 (M) 最接近纳西人 (X)，正如我们之前所展示的，或者图 2 中的两个群体之间预计存在共享或相关的单倍型，我们预计他们拥有一个最近的共同祖先。然而，在网络中并没有看到这样的关系，强烈反对摩梭人和纳西人在母系血统上拥有最近共同祖先的假设。

“This provides strong evidence for the affinity between the Mosuo and Pumi in the maternal lineages.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 17) 这为摩梭人和普米人在母系血统上的亲缘关系提供了有力的证据。

讨论

“This indicates that simply comparing haplogroup frequencies among populations is not sufficient in mtDNA data analyses and sometime even results in large bias. A network analysis can offer more details and higher resolution about population relationships.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 18) 这表明，在线粒体DNA数据分析中，简单地比较人群之间的单倍群频率是不够的，有时甚至会导致较大的偏差。网络分析可以提供有关人口关系的更多细节和更高的分辨率。

“The Mosuo are not really isolated from the Pumi or the Lolo, but rather they are quite possibly a part of Pumi. Therefore, the most probable explanation is that Mosuo started matrilocality not long ago, and a possible reason for abandoning patrilocality is the frequent mercantile travels of their men.” (Lu 等, 2012, p. 18) 摩梭人并不是真正与普米族或彝族隔绝，而是很可能是普米族的一部分。因此，最可能的解释是，摩梭人不久前就开始了母系制，而放弃父系社会的一个可能原因是他们的男人频繁的往来旅行。
