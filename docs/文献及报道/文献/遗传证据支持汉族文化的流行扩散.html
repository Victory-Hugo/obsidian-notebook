介绍

Genetic evidence supports demic diffusion of Han culture

“The spread of culture and language in human populations is explained by two alternative models: the demic diffusion model, which involves mass movement of people; and the cultural diffusion model, which refers to cultural impact between populations and involves limited genetic exchange between them1” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 文化和语言在人群中的传播可以通过两种模型来解释：人口扩散模型，涉及人口的大规模流动；文化扩散模型，指的是人群之间的文化影响(人群之间只有有限的基因交换）。

“whether the diffusion of agriculture and language from the Near East was concomitant with a large movement of farmers” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 欧洲人口聚居的机制长期以来一直存在争议，一个关键问题是来自近东的农业和语言的传播是否伴随着农民的大规模流动。

“Studies on classical genetic markers and microsatellites show that the Han people, like East Asians, are divided into two genetically differentiated groups, northern Han and southern Han6,8, separated approximately by the Yangtze river” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 经典遗传标记和微卫星研究表明，汉族与东亚人一样，分为两个遗传分化的群体，即北方汉族和南方汉族，大约以长江为间隔。

“substantial sharing of Y-chromosome and mitochondrial lineages between the two groups11,12 and the historical records describing the expansion of Han people5 contradict the cultural diffusion model hypothesis of Han expansion” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 两个群体之间 Y 染色体和线粒体谱系的大量共享以及描述汉族扩张的历史记录与汉族扩张的文化扩散模型假设相矛盾。

“southern Hans and northern Hans share similar frequencies of Y-chromosome haplogroups” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 南方汉族和北方汉族的Y染色体单倍群频率相似，其特征是两个携带 M122-C 突变（O3-M122 和 O3e-M134）的单倍群，这些突变在几乎所有研究的汉族人群中都普遍存在。

“M119-C (O1 and O1b) and/or M95-T (O2a and O2a1) (following the nomenclature of the Y Chromosome Consortium)” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 携带 M119-C（O1 和 O1b）和/或 M95-T（O2a 和 O2a1）（遵循 Y 染色体联盟的命名法）的单倍群在南方原住民中普遍存在，在南方汉族中更为常见。

“The results of analysis of molecular variance (AMOVA) further indicate that northern Hans and southern Hans are not significantly different in their Y haplogroups (FST ¼ 0.006, P . 0.05), demonstrating that southern Hans bear a high resemblance to northern Hans in their male lineages.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 分子方差分析（AMOVA）结果进一步表明，北方汉族和南方汉族的Y单倍群没有显着差异，说明南方汉族与北方汉族的相似度较高。

“On the maternal side, however, the mtDNA haplogroup distribution showed substantial differentiation between northern Hans and southern Hans” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 然而，在母系方面，北方汉族和南方汉族的mtDNA单倍群分布存在显着差异。

  1. “The first wave involving 0.9 million (approximately one-sixth of the southern population at that time) occurred during the Western Jin Dynasty (AD 265–316);” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 303) 第一波涉及 90 万人（约占当时南方人口的六分之一）发生在西晋时期（公元 265-316 年）；
  2. “the second migration, more extensive than the first, took place during the Tang Dynasty (AD 618–907);” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 303) 第二次移民比第一次更广泛，发生在唐朝（公元 618-907 年）；
  3. “third wave, including ,5 million immigrants, occurred during the Southern Song Dynasty (AD 1127–1279).” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 303) 第三波移民潮发生在南宋时期（公元 1127-1279 年），共有 500 万移民。

“The overall frequencies of the northern East Asian-dominating haplogroups (A, C, D, G, M8a, Yand Z) are much higher in northern Hans” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 东亚北部主导的单倍群（A、C、D、G、M8a、Yand Z）的总体频率在北方汉族中要高得多。

“In contrast, the frequency of the haplogroups that are dominant lineages (B, F, R9a, R9b and N9a) in southern natives12,14,18 is much higher in southern” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 302) 相比之下，南方原住民中占优势谱系的单倍群（B、F、R9a、R9b 和 N9a）的频率在南方人中要高得多。

“a much more substantial admixture in southern Hans’ female gene pool than in its male counterpart.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 303) 南方汉族女性基因库中的混合成分比男性基因库中的混合成分要多得多。

“We provide two lines of evidence supporting the demic diffusion hypothesis for the expansion of Han culture.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 304) 我们提供了两条证据来支持汉族文化扩张的人口扩散假说。

  1. “First, almost all Han populations bear a high resemblance in Y-chromosome haplogroup distribution, and the result of principal component analysis indicated that almost all Han populations form a tight cluster in their Y chromosome.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 304) 首先，几乎所有汉族人群的Y染色体单倍群分布都具有高度相似性，主成分分析结果表明几乎所有汉族人群的Y染色体都形成紧密的簇。
  2. “Second, the estimated contribution of northern Hans to southern Hans is substantial in both paternal and maternal lineages and a geographic cline exists for mtDNA.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 304) 其次，北方汉族对南方汉族的父系和母系谱系贡献都很大，并且线粒体DNA存在地理谱系。

“It is noteworthy that the expansion process was dominated by males, as is shown by a greater contribution to the Y-chromosome than the mtDNA from northern Hans to southern Hans.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 304) 值得注意的是，扩张过程中男性占主导地位，北方汉族向南方汉族对Y染色体的贡献大于mtDNA。

方法

样本

“871 unrelated anonymous individuals from 17 Han populations were collected across China.” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 304) 收集了来自中国 17 个汉族人群的 871 名无关匿名个体。

“HVS-1 motif and the coding region variations were used to infer haplogroups following the phylogeny of East Asian mtDNAs” (Wen 等, 2004, p. 304) HVS-1 基序和编码区变异用于根据东亚 mtDNA 的系统发育推断单倍群。
